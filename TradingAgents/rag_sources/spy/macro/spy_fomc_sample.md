# FOMC Statement Sample

## Key Facts

- The committee kept policy restrictive.
- Inflation remained above the 2 percent target.
- Labor market conditions remained resilient.

## Why It Matters For SPY

Higher-for-longer rates can pressure SPY valuation multiples, especially because the index has large exposure to long-duration growth stocks.

## Evidence Notes

- Policy stance: restrictive
- Main risk: inflation persistence
- SPY impact: valuation pressure
